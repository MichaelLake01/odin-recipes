Simple Syrup

Lovely and delicious syrup for drinks and desserts!

Learn how to make simple syrup to use in homemade cocktails and other drink recipes. It's easy to make with just two ingredients!

Ingredients

  • white sugar
  • water

  1. Combine

    Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool.

  2. Refrigerate

    Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid; store in the refrigerator for up to one month.
